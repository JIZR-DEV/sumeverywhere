Privacy Policy

SumEverywhere does not collect, transmit or store any personal data or page content outside your device. All text extraction and summarization happens locally on your device. The AI summarizer (Gemini Nano) runs entirely on-device. The extractive fallback is pure JavaScript with no network calls. No browsing history, no page content, and no summaries are ever sent to any server. The only data stored is your preferences (preferred engine, length, format) in browser.storage.local, which remains on your device and can be deleted at any time from the Options page.
